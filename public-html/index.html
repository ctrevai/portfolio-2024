    Dr. Chomchana Trevai (Chom) is an entrepreneur, investor, and ventures builder with over 20 years of experience at the intersection of AI, robotics, and enterprise technology. Previously Head of AI/ML Business for ASEAN at AWS, Chom helped organizations across Southeast Asia transition from piloting to operationalizing machine learning at scale. Before AWS, he held business development and technology leadership roles at Cisco Systems, GE Digital, and ServiceMax in Japan. Chom holds a Ph.D. from The University of Tokyo, where his research focused on cooperative multi-robot systems and autonomous exploration using reaction-diffusion models. He is a frequent speaker on AI, agentic AI, generative AI, and the future of intelligent systems, and is passionate about democratizing AI/ML to drive real-world impact.
